Ninartt is a reflective individual whose artwork expresses the difficulty of handling internal emotions when facing the world around oneself. Her two primary mediums of creativity are through music and two-dimensional art. Musically, she is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in music performance for violin. Illustratively, she prefers to work in the silence of night without distractions, often on sketches or watercolors. To further push her own boundaries, she has recently started accepting commissions to create custom art pieces.
